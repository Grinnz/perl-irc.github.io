irc.perl.org - Rules

  • Reasonable Person Principle Applies.
  • Things Not To Do (these will most likely get you klined/glined):
    • Spamming : Pasting your favorite url repeatedly in many channels
    • Mass flooding
    • Ban/ignore evasion : If a channel/user doesn't want to hear from you, respect that.
    • Threats : Serious threats of physical or network violence will be dealt with immediately and harshly.
    • Logging : Do not log and publish the activity of a channel without the channel's express consent.
  • Don't break the law.
  • Obey the opers. They're here to protect the network and the community as a whole.
